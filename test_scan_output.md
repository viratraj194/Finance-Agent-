### Risk Management & Trading Guidelines
1. **Position Sizing**: Allocate 2-3% of your portfolio to each trade.
2. **Stop Loss**: Set a stop loss at the specified level to limit potential losses.
3. **Risk/Reward Ratio**: Ensure a risk/reward ratio of 1:2 to minimize potential losses and maximize potential gains.
4. **Trading Hours**: Trade during market hours (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM IST) to minimize overnight risks.
5. **Capital Protection**: Prioritize capital protection and adjust position sizes accordingly.
6. **Regular Portfolio Rebalancing**: Rebalance your portfolio regularly to maintain an optimal asset allocation.
7. **Stay Informed**: Stay up-to-date with market news and trends to make informed trading decisions.

By following these guidelines and trade setups, you can potentially maximize your returns while minimizing risks. However, please note that trading in the stock market involves risks, and there are no guarantees of returns. It is essential to do your own research and consult with a financial advisor before making any investment decisions.
